Dear Son___________________

Chapter 1

 

                    I am writing this so you will know a little about the town Hishn and about me.  I hope one day you will return to Hishn and visit me.  What I am doing and what I have to do are both a monumental sacrifice and a monumental joy.  The complexity of the matter you will certainly never completely grasp but I have to try and explain to you as best I can the why and how of the situation.  First let me begin with Hishn.

 

          Hishn is an Elven town that came into existence about 500 years ago.  But older than the town is a temple to Rao.  In fact when Hishn was first beginning to take shape as a struggling “outpost” the temple of Rao already existed.  The town Hishn grew around the temple.  The temple of Rao is cared for by the only humans living in Hishn.  In fact the descendents of the human family have always cared for the temple of Rao and by their accounts the temple has stood since 2CY.  The family is rarely seen in Hishn since the affairs they deal in take place within the gray temple walls.  I have spoken with the current patriarch of the temple of Rao, the place is their home as much as it is a temple, and he tells me his sons’ like all other males in his family go out to seek fortune and a wife.  Then the young men return to the temple of Rao in order to upkeep it and worship Rao.  The current Patriarch is called Olen and I doubt he will be alive, since he is 66 now, when and if I see you again after you leave me.  Olen has 3 sons to take over as Patriarch of the temple of Rao so the line runs strong to this day.

         

          The son’s names are Nethaniel, Nicolbolas and Noah.  Nethaniel I have met and he likes to be called Neth.  He is 14 for a human that is getting close to grown. Neth is growing into a man with values and morals similar if not identical to his father Olen.  Nicolbolas is 20 and Noah is nearing 30 though I am unclear as to how old he is.  I have never actually seen Noah though his brother Neth and the wife of Olen speak of him to me when I visit the temple.  Why am I writing you this?  Why should you care about the temple of Rao?  After all he is a god of humans…

 

         

 

Rao is the god your brother has chose though he is an Elf.  I visit the temple

Once every week to feel closer to my son in his absence.  Numenarh Daerkwood is my firstborn child.  He is tall for an Elf, except for his father whom is the same height, and the tallest in Hishn.  Numenarh is good hearted and careful with words.  Numenarh judges people slowly and carefully.  For Numenarh to call someone friend is not to be taken lightly and from what I know of these days calling him enemy is to be taken soberly.  Numenarh is an adventurer and a risk-taker.  You could call him the hero of the family.  Perenthace is young and is your brother as well…

 

          Perenthace will always be young and childlike but none the less the boy knows things.  Perenthace has been given the gift of prophecy by what hand or fate I do not know. In fact Perenthace told me that you would be a boy.  I take Perenthace at his word and so call you son.  I have never known Perenthace to be wrong about such things as he tells me will happen.  Some things are mundane and others important.  Sometimes he says enigmatic things that I don’t understand.  Just the other day he said: “Numenarh is red and in Harmony with a big “H” and he grows close to thought and action, word and deed.”  I have no idea what that means but I write it here because is shows the way Perenthace relates to the world.

 

          Now something about me: Marahnaiol is my name.  My childhood name was Lia and before I became a Daerkwood my family name was Holimion.  Holimion means Diamonddew.  At age 90 I declared myself Marahnaiol and 2 years later I married your father.  When I was 50 I began to realize I had special gifts.  I have an inborn talent for magic.  I am a sorcerer and not only that I have psionic talents as well.  I developed the abilities of sorcerer innately but the psionic talents were less easy to control and to harness though both come naturally to me I suppose.  I am 214 years old and soon to be 215.  That must seem old to you but it isn’t so much for an Elven person.  I love apples and a chance meeting in the Vesve caused me to meet your father…

 

          I was traveling and seeing the world when I grew hungry and I noticed I was coming into a place where majestic apple trees lined the forest.  After picking some I was confronted by your father who told me that I was

 

 

Stealing.  I asked how I could be stealing when these apples were in The Vesve and free for everyone?  Well I got my answer.  About 150 years ago the family your father belongs to was granted a fife of land in the Vesve and so they grew apples and were owners of an orchard.  This made your family a generous living.  Recently though a fire swept through the Vesve and the orchard your family had was utterly destroyed.  But your father is well to do and a seasoned adventurer and Wizard so you will want for nothing as you grow up.  If ever your father should meet hardship he knows he can count on me to assist in all the ways that I can…but do not concern yourself with these things because your life will be filled with plenty.  I don’t know how old you will be when you begin reading this but I asked your father to let you begin at age 12 and sooner if you have too many questions.  I don’t have much more time to write just now as I have a lecture to give at The School of Tal concerning some ancient text I have been translating as of late.  I wanted this to be, what I write you, a kind of unfolding story and so read as much or as little as you like as it goes along.  I will divide it into Chapters beginning with most obviously this as Chapter 1.  I know I have not explained much but I not only write you in my spare-time but also in secret.  Do not worry all will be explained in due time as these pages unfold.

 

 

          Until I write you again dear son________Marahnaiol
