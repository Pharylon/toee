Zach's Ranger

(a variant of the Monte Cook Ranger)

HD:  d8

Skill Points per Level:  6+Int Modifier

Class Skills:  As per the standard Ranger

Bonus Feats:  The Ranger may select any of the following feats with his bonus feats:  Ambidexterity, Blind-Fight, Combat Reflexes, Dodge (Mobility, Spring Attack), Exotic Weapon Proficiency, Expertise (Improved Disarm, Improved Trip, Whirlwind Attack), Mounted Combat (Mounted Archery, Trample, Ride-By Attack, Spirited Charge), Point Blank Shot (Far Shot, Precise Shot, Rapid Shot, Shot on the Run), Quick Draw, Two-Weapon Fighting (Improved Two-Weapon Fighting), Weapon Finesse,  Weapon Focus, or any feat with the words "favored enemy" in their text (this includes many feats from Masters of the Wild).
