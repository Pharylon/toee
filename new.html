November 25, 2002:  Since I now have at least a few feats for each faction and each of the factols done, I've decided to officially launch the site.  Nothing much will change as a result - I still plan frequent updates, but it just means I'll be pimping it more around the D&D community. ;)  Basically, I now think it's far enough along that I'm confident in showing it off to others, and beginning to announce updates far and wide.

November 21, 2002:  The Factols are finished.  Rowan Darkwood, Darius, Erin Montgomery, and Karan have all been added.  Additionally, the Dreamer feat has been added.

November 20, 2002:  Factol Rhys has been added.

November 11, 2002:  Character histories for Sarin, Alishon Nelisia, and Beringe are new

November 8, 2002:  Added stats for Lethea and Lesander, which were submitted by William Bryan (Estavans Lackey on the Realms of Evil messageboards).  

November 7, 2002:  Character histories for Lhar, Pentar, Skall, Hashkar, and Bria Tomay have been added.

November 4, 2002:  OK, the other factols haven't been added, but I did add a histories for Terrance and Ambar.  The rest of the factols will follow.

November 3, 2002:  It's a big day.  The factions are all now stat'd out, except for Rhys, Darius, and Karan.  I hope to have them up tomorrow, along with a short text description of them.  Besides that, the Bleakniks, Faction Infiltrators, Sinker Swordmaster, and Justicar prestige classes are new.

October 22, 2002:  Sweet, I have message boards now.

October 16, 2002:  Searchers in the Dark, a Sensate prestige class.
