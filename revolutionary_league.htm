the Revolutionary League

 

NOTE:  This is faction has only the bare essentials for playing in 3rd edition currently present.  For a look at a completed faction, see either the Doomguard, Godsmen, or Harmonium.

new feats

Deception [General, Anarchist]
Anarchists are skilled with those abilities that allow them to infiltrate other factions. 
Prerequisites:  Membership in the Revolutionary League
Benefit:  You gain a +1 bonus to Bluff, Disguise, Forgery, and Innuendo.  In adddition, you may choose one to always be a class skill for you.
Special:  You may take this feat multiple times, each time you do, choose another skill to become a class skill for you.

Expert Infiltration [General, Bleak Cabal]
You are an expert at infiltrating other factions
Prerequisite: Deception
Benefit:  You gain a +5 bonus to Bluff, Disguise, Forgery and Innuendo checks when trying to convince someone you belong to a particular faction.
