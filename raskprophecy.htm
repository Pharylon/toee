Rask's Prophecy

In the common year of 91, there shall be born an elf with hate and malice in his heart and veins.  Shunned by the elven community, he shall take to the wild, and learn the ways of the barbarians so that he may wreak havoc and revenge on those whom he believed wronged him.  For Four Hundred Years, none would best him in battle, but the day will come when battle would not be enough for him.  Seeking more, he shall call upon Nerull for help and loose his life in the dark arts so that he may gain unlife and feed on the living. 

On that night there will be born a human whose gift in fighting the undead will be mighty.  He shall grow and learn ways of fighting undeath that few, if any, have before.  In time, the Elf will learn of this, and try to stop the Man, and so they are destined to battle, though only one is destined to survive.

This is what I see.  This is the future.

- from The Collected Prophecies of Erza Mithrin
