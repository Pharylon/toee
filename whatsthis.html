This is my Planescape site, dedicated to the conversion of the Planescape factions to 3rd edition, and to coming up with entirely new ideas and twists on the subject.

Now, does that mean you have to be a Planescape fan to take advantage of the site?  Nope.  It sure helps, but it's not required.  At the beginning of each faction's section, there's a little blurb on each faction's philosophical outlook to get you aquatinted if you've never played Planescape, and to refresh your memory if it's been a while. 

And even if you've never played Planescape and don't ever intend to, you can drop these factions, or even just the individual feats, prestige classes, and characters directly into your own campaign.  I've tried to make things extremely portable.

The actual mechanics system for the factions is simple:  joining a faction has no immediate benefits, other than having a group of friends, and a place to hang your hat when your down on your luck.  However, joining one does allow you to begin picking up faction feats and skills.

One last thing.  I strongly suggest that as a house rule, all residents of the planes have access to the Cosmopolitan feat out of the Forgotten Realms Campaign setting (Cosmopolitan gives you a +2 bonus to any one non-exclusive skill and makes that skill a class skill for you from now on).

Enjoy!

 

- Zach Shuford
